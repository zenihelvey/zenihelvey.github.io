Charming Cafes in Winston-Salem

Explore the coziest spots to enjoy coffee, tea, and Southern hospitality.

Mozelle's Fresh Southern Bistro - Mozelle's Fresh Southern Bistro is a popular spot known for its farm-to-table Southern dishes. This cozy restaurant embodies true Southern hospitality, offering classics like shrimp and grits, fried chicken, and collard greens. With a focus on local ingredients, Mozelle's menu brings fresh flavors and creative spins on traditional favorites.

Bobby Boy Bakeshop - Satisfy your sweet tooth at Bobby Boy Bakeshop, a cozy bakery known for its artisanal pastries, cakes, and bread. This charming spot makes delicious treats from scratch with top-quality ingredients and a love for baking.
