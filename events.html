Festival of Lights at Tanglewood Park - Each year, Tanglewood Park turns into a sparkling Winter Wonderland with over a million twinkling lights. The Festival of Lights runs nightly from November through New Year’s Day, open from 6-11 p.m. Visitors can enjoy the stunning displays and festive tunes from interactive musical setups, featuring original music by UNC School of the Arts students. With over 80 displays along the scenic route, it’s a magical holiday experience.

Gears & Guitars - Gears & Guitars returns to Innovation Quarter from May 24-26, 2024, for its eleventh annual event. This exciting weekend combines world-class bike racing, community rides, and live music, making it the ultimate party on two wheels.

Winston-Salem Open - Winston-Salem hosts the Winston-Salem Open each August at the Wake Forest University Tennis Complex. As the final ATP World Tour 250 event before the US Open, the WSO attracts over 40 of the world's top players competing for the title. Past players include stars like Sam Querrey, Steve Johnson, and Pablo Carreno Busta. The event takes place at the Wake Forest Tennis Complex, next to Truist Field.
